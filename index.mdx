---

YT Reader Mode transforms how you interact with YouTube by overlaying the native YouTube AI Chat panel directly on top of the video player. Instead of watching videos passively, you can read AI-generated summaries, ask questions, and explore content — all without distractions from the video itself.

## Get started in minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Clone or download the repository">
    Get the source code from [GitHub](https://github.com/meyer-pidiache/yt-reader-mode) to your local machine.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Load the extension in your browser">
    Open `chrome://extensions/`, enable **Developer mode**, click **Load unpacked**, and select the project folder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to a YouTube video">
    Open any YouTube video that has the AI Chat feature available. YT Reader Mode activates automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize your experience">
    Click the extension icon in your browser toolbar to configure auto-activate behavior and set an initial prompt.
  </Step>
</Steps>
